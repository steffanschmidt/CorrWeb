Materials Science and Theoretical Knowledge in General

Materials science is the area I specialized in and have a broad knowledge about. This ranges from biological environment with biomaterials and the chemistry of life to the derivation and understanding of the equations governing materials and their properties. A short list of the most important subjects can be seen here.

  • Surface and Bulk properties, Coatings and characterization thereof.
  • Solid state physics and the properties and theory of metals.
  • Electric and magnetic fields, thermodynamics and mechanics.
  • Inorganic chemistry - synthesizing new materials.
  • Polymers and Soft Materials in general - synthesis and characterization
  • Bioinorganic materials, Biomaterials and Biochemistry.

Some of these subjects will be covered below. In practice I worked with synthesis and characterization of inorganic materials as can be seen in Chemistry.
