Personal Philosophy

In general I believe that continous learning and reflection are very important aspects both to grow personally and professionally. I spend a great deal of time on the former with programming and working on Practical Projects. I always attempt to keep up to date, and I start to feel stuck when I do the same thing over and over again. Therefore it is very important to me that the work I do has either meaning or a concrete outcome either in the form of product or new discoveries (eg. in natural science). In other words development is one of the keywords for me. Regarding reflection then it has a two-fold meaning in this case. Namely self-reflection and scientific reflection. The latter is the one I enjoy the most is reflecting on consequences of say some theory or assumption in natural science. For example if I attempt to develop a theory for corrosion, and I assume that I can describe all external factors on it, how will this show in measurements and how will it affect the outcome, and if I change this certain parameter what will happen i.e. what is the physical interpretation of this value. However self-reflection is of critical importance to me, since it is basically everything else. For me it is mostly in the form how other perceive me and what effect some action will have. In general and especially during the entirety of my job search, I spend a lot of time on this.

I do wish to come across as the very down to earth person I am, despite my occational obsession with natural sciences and development. Hopefully I can convince you of that with the sections below and on this site in general.

Plants

I love to see things grow and flourish in general, and therefore I do very much enjoy working with plants and taking care of them. The process of planting is enjoyable and relaxing to me and I like a wide variety of plants. However I am really fond of succulents because partly they are easy to take care of and partly due the fact they come in a wide variety. The simple look is also appealing to me rather than having flower bearing plants. I also like cacti for the same reasons. Therefore I have been gathering myself a small greenhouse worth of succulent plants over the last couple of years due to offsets from my plants. Other than that I have about 60 strawberry plants on my terrace, some of which can seen here just before budding last year. Unfortunately due to the space constraints their yield is not that great, since they need a lot more room to grow. Nevertheless the outcome that do come is greatly appreciated and tastes delicious.

On a sidenote I have a small dream that some time in the future I will have myself a garden, where I expand my collection of plants and grow things for cooking.

Cooking and Baking

Cooking is one of the things I do really like doing, hence I put a lot energy into it. On a daily basis this to me is where I have time to relax or spend time to reflect and/or generate new ideas. It varies from day to day whether or not I can to spend a lot of time on it, but in general I do like to make a proper evening meal every day. Other than that I attempt to vary my cooking and learn new recipes in order to mix things up from time to time. I personally like to have a basic recipe for a certain thing and then change that rather than finding new ones every single time, I want to try something new. In any case cooking is a passion of mine and the more stuff I can make from scratch the happier I am.

Apart from cooking I decide to bake once a while. It is usually something I do, when I feel like it, or if I have an appointment where say a cake would be nice. It brings all the same comforts for me as cooking does, however one can only eat so much cake. Therefore usually when I make a cake I do it out of interest rather than eating it on my own. In other words it is not unlikely that I try to get rid of it by sharing it with friends, family and/or colleagues.
